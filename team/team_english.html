Introduction
The laboratory has 4 doctoral students (including 2 international students) and 17 master students. The main research directions of the laboratory are big data analysis, bioinformatics technology, wise information technology of medical, social network analysis, etc. The laboratory has a strong academic atmosphere, solidarity and mutual assistance, and a number of high-level academic achievements are mass-produced every year. Two of the graduate students trained in the past five years have won national scholarships, and the team students have the opportunity to visit abroad.
